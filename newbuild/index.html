The New Dolphins Hall

Tetbury's Community Hall and Recreation Ground

Much Loved But Old

The Dolphins Hall was built over fifty years ago, adding a much-needed community facility alongside The Recreation Ground, to be managed by the same Trust. In that time it has been well used and well loved; a place for community events, gatherings, celebrations, casting of votes, sport, regular meetings, singing, dancing, laughter, music and drama (of all kinds). Tetbury may be technically a small town, but the Dolphins Hall has been, and will continue to be, our “village hall”. The hall and the Rec together are a special place at the heart of our community.

Planning Application

Given the current unusual and uncertain world which we find ourselves living in, and one where positive news stories seem hard to come by, we are delighted to announce that yesterday evening, the Dolphins Recreation Centre submitted the full planning application for the Redevelopment Project!

New Build Planning Application

We are delighted to announce that yesterday evening, 3rd April 2020, the Dolphins Recreation Centre submitted the full planning application for the Redevelopment Project! This marks the end of a challenging and detailed design and review process, spanning almost eighteen months, and which has involved five public consultation events and two online surveys. We’ve had plenty of feedback which we have incorporated into our thinking, so we are hopeful we have listened and got this right!
