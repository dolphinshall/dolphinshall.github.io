THE NEW DOLPHINS HALL

New Build Drop In Consultation - December 15th 10am to 2pm

November 21, 2019

We appreciate it has been a while since we provided everyone with an update on the progress of the Redevelopment Project so as we approach the end of the year, we felt it was an appropriate time to let you know what has been going on behind the scenes the past few months.

The trustees have been working hard to incorporate the feedback we received from those of you who attend our last round of consultation within the plans. We’ve also been through a very positive pre-planning process with the local Planning and Conservation Officers who are supportive of our plans and given us some excellent feedback on how we can improve them further. We’ve also put together a business plan for how the new facility can operate once it has been built.

  This means, we are now in a position where we are beginning to prepare our full planning application to be sent to Cotswold District Council. Before we do this though, we want the opportunity to share the latest designs with all of you and give you the chance to provide any further feedback which we can incorporate into our final submission.

  We would therefore be delighted if you could join us on Sunday 15th December between 10AM and 2PM to enjoy a mince pie with the trustees and talk over the latest plans. We hope to see as many of you there as possible.
