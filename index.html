The Dolphins Hall

Tetbury's Community Hall and Recreation Ground

Dolphins Hall is a modern hall boasting a wide range of facilities including room for 120 people, kitchen, stage, car park & more. Next to the hall is the recreation ground that is available for the enjoyment and use of all the people of Tetbury.
