June 23, 2020

The Dolphins Recreation Centre AGM

The Dolphins Hall Management Committee AGM would typically be held sometime between April and June. The public at the AGM would elect six resident trustees to serve until the next AGM, and the trustees present the accounts to those present. At last years AGM there were nearly one hundred people. Current social distancing rules make holding a meeting of this size impractical. Also, traditional paper-based secret ballots are quite high contact events, which is far from desirable at this time.

March 22, 2020

Covid-19 Update - Hall Closed Until End of April

In light of the amended guidance from the government announced on the 20th March 2020 the trustees have now decided to close the Dolphins Hall until at least the end of April 2020. All bookings at this time will be cancelled and refunded. The trustees will continue to monitor the government advice and review the closure again if things change. The sad reality is the closure will probably need to go on beyond this original period.
