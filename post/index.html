June 1, 2021

Annual Report April 2020 to March 2021

Introduction This has been a strange year. COVID-19 has stopped almost everything the trust normally does, yet the trustees have possibly been the busiest we have ever been. Not having to focus on the day-to-day activities of running the hall has allowed time for the trustees to correct and put in place some of the things that have been allowed to slip in the past. That means, as we now reach the end of our financial year and move into the next, we do so in a stronger, and maybe more organised position, than the trust has ever been in.

We Need Your Help

Since the 29th June 2020, the Dolphins Recreation Centre have been attempting to work with Tetbury Town Council to allow for the forwarding of the S106 funding which the Council holds for the purposes of improving the existing facilities at the Dolphins Hall and Recreation Ground. You will have noticed that for the last three months we have made no public announcements on how this process was going in order to allow these discussions to take their course without any social media storm upsetting them or impacting progress.

Planning Permission Granted

Wow, a great week continues! The Dolphins Recreation Centre are delighted to confirm today we received confirmation from Cotswold District Council that they are supportive of the Redevelopment Project and have approved our planning application in full! This has been a long road for the trustees and marks another significant milestone for the progress of the project.
