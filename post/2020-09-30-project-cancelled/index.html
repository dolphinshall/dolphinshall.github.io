Project Cancelled

September 30, 2020

PROJECT CANCELLED…

…are not words we will say lightly, but may just be the ones we are forced to say if Tetbury Town Council (TTC) continue in the manner they are currently.

• Cotswold District Council a number of years ago awarded the Dolphins Recreation Centre over £500,000 of Section 106 funding;

• Eleven weeks ago, TTC’s Finance & Scrutiny committee unanimously agreed to forward these funds to use on the Community, Youth & Sport Complex (which is the first phase of our Redevelopment Project);

• Despite this, the funds have still not been received;

• Every day TTC delays, increases the likelihood of a further £225,000 of funding being lost;

• TTC refused our requests to put the forwarding of the funds on the agenda at the latest Council Meeting on Monday 28th September;

• They have said they will hold a meeting to discuss it, they just don’t know when yet. Given the potential consequences of their delays, this isn’t acceptable;

• We are now in a position, due to the delays caused by TTC, that anything other than the funds being released immediately for the Community, Youth & Sport Complex as previously agreed, will see the entire Redevelopment Project cancelled;

• Alternatively we could ask you, the people of Tetbury, to decide whether you want to sell off part of the Recreation Ground to fund the shortfall caused by TTC’s failure to forward the funds;

• That though would be disastrous for Tetbury – having to sell off land to generate funds, while funds that could have been used, are returned to Cotswold District Council, and eventually the developers, due to a combination of TTC’s incompetence and their lack of authority to use the funds for anything else;

• Should TTC’s actions result in the project being cancelled, this will see the Tetbury community and local businesses miss out on over £2 million of investment and revenue.

This is your facility, your future and your Redevelopment Project. For the past two years while TTC have obstructed the project at every opportunity, you have been resolute in your support. You deserve better than how Tetbury Town Council are treating you and the facilities they themselves provide the town. If we leave it to them, and they continue to dither and delay, we are doomed, meaning we need your help and support again. We need to work together to save the project. We’ll let you know what we need shortly. We will also post further information and evidence to show you how TTC, and specific Councillors, have overstepped their remit, lied and issued threats in an attempt to derail the project.

Together, we can - and will - get through this.
