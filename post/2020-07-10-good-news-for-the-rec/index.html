Good News For The Rec

July 10, 2020

Even in lockdown it has been a busy time for trustees as we not only continue work on the Redevelopment Project, but we look at other ways of delivering on our commitment to the people of Tetbury to make the facilities the best they can be.

After years of neglect, we started work last year on improving the upkeep and maintenance of the Recreation Ground. The eagle eyed among you will have noticed the work we started last week to improve the facilities enjoyed by everyone out on the Recreation Ground.

This has been possible thanks to the great work of Paul Jones, trustee and Chair of Grounds Committee who has managed to secure a five year funding plan of over £40,000.

He was able to do this by demonstrating how the trustees have transformed the running of the charity in the last eighteen months to deliver a sustainable business model which encompasses all aspects of the facilities provided by the Dolphins Recreation Centre.

These funds will be invested directly into all the green space which we own and manage to improve the experience of visiting and using the facility for all our many user groups.

Thanks Paul and as ever, if you have any ideas on how we can improve the facilities or feedback on a recent visit, please do let us know by emailing chair@dolphinshall.org
