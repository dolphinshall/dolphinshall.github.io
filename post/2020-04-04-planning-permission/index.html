NEWS AND ANNOUNCEMENTS

New Build Planning Application

April 4, 2020

Given the current unusual and uncertain world which we find ourselves living in, and one where positive news stories seem hard to come by, we are delighted to announce that yesterday evening, the Dolphins Recreation Centre submitted the full planning application for the Redevelopment Project!

For more information please see the full announcement

There have been many challenges to overcome to get to this point, and it is not an understatement to say the kind words and supportive conversations have been all we’ve had to keep us going at points, so our heartfelt thanks go out to the community of Tetbury. Our intention is very much to deliver the facilities which you have asked us for and which we feel you absolutely deserve.

Stay safe everyone, keep smiling and hopefully the approval of our planning application can be another positive news story we can share with you all in the upcoming weeks.
