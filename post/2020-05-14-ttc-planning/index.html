Tetbury Town Council Planning Committee

May 14, 2020

Very sad that tonight Tetbury Town Council’s Planning Committee failed to support the Dolphins Hall Redevelopment Project and decided to object to our planning application. Delays to the planning put the project at risk due to time limits on certain funds.

Their concerns on which their objections were based were either factually inaccurate or over points that the trustees had discussed with the Cotswold District Council Planning Officer and Conservation Officers prior to submitting the planning application.

Councillors Colin Pearce, Mark Wood, Martin Lea, Rodney Smith and Peter Coleman all voted to object. Councillor Judith Taylor voted not to object. Councillor Sue Brown was unable to vote due to her involvement as a trustee.
