New Website and Booking System

So today we release an update to our website. This new site will allow us to provide more frequent information. Most importantly it now also incorporates and links to a new booking system. The new system will enable you to book the Dolphins Hall online and pay using your credit or debit card. The system has only just been configured so there may be a glitch here and there. If you try to book and something goes wrong, please do get in touch here and let us know and we will sort it out as quickly as we can.
