NEWS AND ANNOUNCEMENTS

Announcement

October 7, 2020

In light of the recent accusations in the latest post on Tetbury Town Councils Facebook Page, we would like to say we take every complaint of bullying and inappropriate behaviour seriously and will investigate and take suitable action to correct any wrongs that have been done.

However, to date, we have not received an official complaint from any members of Tetbury Town Council and their public statement is, in fact, the first time we have heard about it. In consideration of this very public complaint, we have requested that the Mayor, Councillor Ann Pearce present us with the evidence of her claim that bullying has been taking place by a member of the DRC in online public forums so that we can take appropriate action. If she cannot support this very serious accusation then we have requested that she withdraw her statement alongside an apology.
